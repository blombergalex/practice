kitesurfing lessons auckland & matakana coast

The best way to start your kiteboarding adventures is by getting some quality kitesurfing lessons. Our team at Blue Adventures believes in customer satisfaction, and our teaching technique has proven to be extremely successful.

IKO-Affiliated and Safety Approved

Blue Adventures is a Worksafe accredited kite surfing school and Auckland's only IKO- affiliated Kite Centre.

Qualified kitesurfing instructors are trained to teach using IKO's safe progressive method and our kiteboarding lessons are IKO certified.

View our team of instructors here.

Small group sizes

Our kitesurf lessons are run in small groups with a student to instructor ratio of 1:1 or 2:1. This way, your qualified kitesurfing instructor can tailor the kitesurfing lessons to your individual learning needs.

Quality and Easy-To-Use Gear

Step-by-step instructions and the latest Naish and Ozone kiteboarding equipment will ensure you progress easily and safely while learning this addictive sport! The use of all kitesurfing gear, impact vests and helmets is included in our lesson price.

Once you have completed our 2-Day kitesurfing course, you might want to purchase your own kitesurfing equipment. We are here to help you to find the right gear, and are offering a $300 lesson voucher with every kitesurfing equipment package purchased.

Great Lesson Locations

With no crowds and flat and shallow waters, we are teaching at kitesurfing locations, which provide excellent learning conditions. Being a fully mobile kitesurfing school means that we will be at the best location for the conditions.
